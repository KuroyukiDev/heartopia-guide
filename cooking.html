Shared Kitchen Layout (The Hub)

The "Square Formation" Concept:

Arrange four stoves in a 2x2 cluster. This reduces movement frame-data when switching between cooking animations and storage retrieval.

  • Position A (The Farmer): Plots situated on the perimeter for fast collection.
  • Position B (The Chef): 4 Stoves placed in a 2x2 grid in the center.
  • Position C (The Logistics): Storage crates placed directly behind stoves for instant access.
  • Advantage: Minimum travel time between inventory and stove for all 4 players.
